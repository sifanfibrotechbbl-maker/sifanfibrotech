Who We Are

Welcome to Sifan Fibrotech, At Sifan Fibrotech, we specialize in manufacturing high-quality fiber, FRP (Fiber Reinforced Plastic) products designed to meet modern industrial and commercial needs. Our goal is simple — to deliver durable, reliable, and cost-effective fiber solutions without compromising on quality.that make your work easier and your results better.


Founded by Chand Shekh, Sifan Fibrotech was started with a clear vision: to provide dependable FRP products that perform well in tough conditions and last for years. With hands-on industry experience and deep knowledge of FRP manufacturing, the foundation of our company is built on quality, trust, and practical solutions.


With modern technology and a skilled team, we design and manufacture products like FRP motor boxes, panels, safety covers, and custom fiber solutions that can handle tough weather and heavy usage.


We work with premium raw materials and proven manufacturing techniques to ensure every product is strong, lightweight, and long-lasting. From customized FRP products to standard FRP solutions, we focus on precision, strength, and finish in every project we handle.What makes Fibrotech different is our commitment to customer satisfaction. We believe in understanding our client’s requirements clearly and delivering products that match their expectations perfectly. Timely delivery, transparent communication, and consistent quality are the values we work by.


As a growing company, we are constantly improving our processes, upgrading our technology, and expanding our product range to serve a wider range of industries. Whether you need fiber products for industrial, commercial, or customized applications, Sifan Fibrotech is a name you can trust.


We are a team of skilled professionals with hands-on experience in FRP product manufacturing. Our knowledge of materials, molds, and production techniques allows us to deliver products that are both strong and lightweight, suitable for various industrial and commercial applications.


we believe that quality is not an option — it is a responsibility. Every product we manufacture goes through careful planning, proper material selection, and strict quality checks. This ensures that what reaches our customers meets their expectations and performs well over time.
