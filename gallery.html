Industrial

We specialize in industrial FRP works, delivering strong, lightweight, and corrosion-resistant solutions for various industries. Our services include manufacturing and installation of FRP tanks, ducts, gratings, panels, and custom-designed FRP products as per client requirements. With a focus on quality, durability, and safety, we ensure our FRP solutions perform reliably even in harsh industrial environments.

Automobiles

We manufacture and supply FRP automobile products such as bus bodies and different vehicle body parts. Our FRP components are lightweight, strong, and rust-free, which helps in better vehicle performance and longer life. We design our products with proper finishing and durability to meet the daily use requirements of commercial and passenger vehicles.

Defence Product

We offer a wide range of FRP defence products designed to perform reliably in extreme and demanding conditions. Our defence solutions include lightweight FRP shelters, portable cabins, instrument carrying boxes, and customised FRP containers used by defence and para-military forces. These products are engineered for high strength, weather resistance, and easy transportation, making them suitable for high-altitude areas and harsh climates. With a strong focus on durability, safety, and precision, our FRP defence products support critical defence operations with long-lasting performance.
